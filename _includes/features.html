Caseload

Easily manage and prioritise your caseload.

Infinity makes it easy to focus on what really matters. Filter your list to view patients from a single team or multiple teams at once. Sorting your list allows you to prioritise your patients, so you can manage your most important patients first.

Tasks

Create, track and prioritise clinical tasks.

Quickly record tasks on-the go, wherever or whenever they arise. Including due dates and times allows you to prioritise your most urgent tasks across your entire caseload or for just a single patient at a time.

Collaboration

Seamlessly share and handover with your team.

Infinity makes it easy to manage your team members and their roles. Sharing a patient with your team allows your colleagues to access and update information in real-time, ensuring the whole team is working together to provide the best possible care for your patients.

Notifications

Receive alerts and reminders.

Push notifications ensure you are always up-to-date and nothing is ever missed. The notification log allows you to review updates for your entire caseload, in a single view.
